Due to its highly syncretic nature, Latin American music encompasses a wide variety of styles, including influential genres such as bachata, bossa nova, merengue, rumba, salsa, samba, son, and tango. It also encompasses Latin American styles that have originated in the United States such as salsa and Tejano.
